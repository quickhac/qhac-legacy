Grade gradient (power) scale

Grades in QuickHAC are colored in according to the severity of their score. More or darker color indicates a lower grade. This allows for you to be able to pick out poor grades at a glance and quickly figure out what you need to improve. You can control the threshold for the colorization of grades by adjusting power level.

Hue

You can also change the colors that your grades show up as. The scale goes from red to yellow to green to blue to violet and all the way back to red, going through every possible hue in the visible color spectrum.
